SwimIQ - what to double-click (Desktop\StrokeIQ)

1) FIRST (always when something is missing / outdated):
   CLICK-ME-FIRST.bat
   (same as GET-LATEST-FIXED-APP.bat)

2) Elite analysis error ("Supabase is not configured for storage download"):
   FIX-STORAGE.bat

3) Put the REAL Flutter app on https://swimiqapp.com:
   PUBLISH-SWIMIQAPP-COM.bat
   Then upload swimiq\build\swimiq-web-godaddy.zip to GoDaddy public_html and Extract.

Do NOT upload the old website\ brochure folder as the homepage.
Do NOT use Streamlit for coaches.
