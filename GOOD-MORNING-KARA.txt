============================================================

Goal for short clips: about 30-60 seconds, then a full coaching
breakdown (strengths, what to fix, drills, next-race cues).

Do this only:

  1. Double-click   START-SWIMIQ-WITH-ELITE.bat
  2. Leave the Elite black window OPEN
  3. Sign in on localhost (127.0.0.1) — NOT swimiqapp.com
  4. Elite tab → Run Elite Analysis on a short clip
  5. Watch the progress % move
  6. Open Summary + Coaching tabs for the full breakdown

If the black banner about 127.0.0.1:8080 appears:
  Elite window was closed. Run START-SWIMIQ-WITH-ELITE.bat again.

You should get coaching tips even if Gemini has a hiccup.
Gemini key (optional upgrade): GEMINI_API_KEY=... in swimiq\.env
then restart with START-SWIMIQ-WITH-ELITE.bat.

Do NOT hunt for other .bat files first.
