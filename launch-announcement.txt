SWIMIQ LAUNCH ANNOUNCEMENT — FULL VERSION
==========================================

Hey everyone — I'm Aspyn, founder of SwimIQ.

I'm excited to share that we're in the final stretch before launching SwimIQ on iPhone and Android.

SwimIQ is a swim performance passport built for competitive swimmers, parents, and coaches. Our tagline says it plainly: Built in the Water. Driven by Possibility.

Here's what you'll be able to do at launch:

Athlete Passport — Your swimmer's home base: profile, team and coach info, readiness status, highest USA Swimming cut, and what to focus on next.

SwimIQ Score — A single progress number that grows as you log sessions, hit personal bests, and chase goals.

Training tools — Log practices and meets, track personal bests (with automatic PB detection), set time goals, and see progress charts over time.

USA Swimming standards — Built-in 2024–2028 motivational times (B through AAAA), so every session, goal, and meet result shows where you stand.

Video Lab + AI Coach — Upload race footage and get a structured coaching report: what the video shows, top practice priorities, specific drills, estimated time savings, and meet-day notes — written in language families can actually use.

More is on the way inside the passport — SwimDNA, Race Intelligence, and a Recruiting Center — but the core experience is real and ready.

We're aiming to launch in the coming weeks if everything stays on track.

See the latest build and progress here:
https://briezy2014.github.io/StrokeIQ/

Thank you to everyone who's believed in this from the start. We can't wait to put SwimIQ in swimmers' hands.

Stay tuned.

— Aspyn
