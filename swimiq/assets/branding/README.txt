SwimIQ branding — three short file names
========================================

*** LOGIN PAGE USES icon.png — bundled in repo; replace with your 512x512 app icon if needed ***

1) icon.png   (REQUIRED — login)
   Square app picture — 512 x 512 px.

2) banner.png (RECOMMENDED — dashboard & tab strip)
   Wide tab strip — bundled in repo; replace with your ~1200 x 400 px banner.
   Shows full-width across the top of Dashboard and every tab.

3) mark.png or SwimIQ_Mark.PNG (OPTIONAL)
   Tight swimmer mark — used centered when banner is missing.

After adding PNGs: close Chrome, run KARA-SEE-UPDATES-NOW.bat
