2. Information we collect
• Account information: email address, display name, password, and authentication identifiers from our secure sign-in service
• Athlete profiles: name, birthday, gender, team, coach, school, graduation year, recruiting notes, and profile photo you choose to upload
• Training logs, meet results, goals, schedules, and personal bests you enter
• Uploaded swim videos, associated metadata (stroke, distance, course, notes), and AI analysis results
• Device and usage data needed to operate the app (crash logs, session identifiers)
• Subscription and billing status when you purchase a paid plan through Apple or Google

3. How we use information
• Provide swim tracking, standards comparisons, passport tools, and coaching features
• Run AI video analysis when you explicitly request it and consent
• Secure your account and sync data across devices
• Improve reliability and fix bugs
• Comply with law and enforce our Terms

4. How we share information
• Cloud hosting and authentication providers that help us run SwimIQ
• Google cloud AI when you run Video Lab AI analysis (video + context you submit)
• Payment processors (Apple App Store, Google Play) for subscriptions
• Law enforcement or regulators when required by law

We do not sell personal information.

5. Data retention
We retain your data while your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting privacy@swimiqapp.com.

6. Security
We use industry-standard encryption in transit and access controls on our servers. No method is 100% secure.

7. Children's privacy (COPPA)
SwimIQ is designed for competitive swimmers ages 8 through 30.

Ages 8–12 (under 13): A parent or legal guardian must create and control the account, log in, and consent to this Privacy Policy on the athlete's behalf. The parent must also approve AI video analysis before any video is sent for processing.

Ages 13–30: Athletes may create and use their own account with appropriate parental involvement where required by local law.

We collect only information needed to provide the service. Parents may review, delete, or refuse further collection of a child's information by contacting privacy@swimiqapp.com.

8. Your choices
• Access, update, or delete profile data in the app
• Decline AI video analysis (non-AI features remain available on applicable plans)
• Sign out or delete your account

9. International users
Our servers are in the United States. By using SwimIQ you consent to processing in the U.S.

10. Changes
We may update this policy. We will revise the "Last updated" date and, where required, provide additional notice in the app.
