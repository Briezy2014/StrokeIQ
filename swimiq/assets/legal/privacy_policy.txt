1. Who we are
SwimIQ ("we," "us") is operated by [Your Company Legal Name] ("Company"). This policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the SwimIQ mobile application and related services.

Contact: privacy@swimiq.app

2. Information we collect
Account information: email address, display name, and authentication identifiers from our secure sign-in service.

Athlete profile: name, birthday, gender, team, coach, school, graduation year, recruiting notes, and profile photo you choose to upload.

Performance data: training sessions, meet results, goals, schedules, and personal bests you enter.

Video content: swim videos you upload to Video Lab, associated metadata (stroke, distance, course, notes), and AI analysis results.

Device & usage: app version, basic diagnostic logs needed to operate the service.

3. How we use information
We use information to:
• Provide dashboards, logs, standards comparisons, and athlete passport features
• Generate SwimIQ AI coaching reports when you request analysis
• Store and sync your data securely across your devices
• Improve reliability and security of the app

4. Third-party service providers
We use trusted processors, including:
• Cloud infrastructure providers (hosting, database, authentication, file storage)
• Google cloud AI services (video coaching analysis — video and context you submit are processed by Google as our subprocessors when you run SwimIQ AI analysis)

We do not sell your personal information.

5. AI processing (Apple App Store disclosure)
When you tap "Run AI Swim Analysis," your video and related swimmer context are transmitted to our servers and then to Google’s AI services to generate coaching feedback. We ask for your explicit in-app consent before the first transmission. You may decline and still use non-AI features.

6. Data retention
We retain your data while your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting privacy@swimiq.app. We may retain limited records where required by law.

7. Children and teen athletes (ages 8–30)
SwimIQ serves youth, junior, collegiate, and post-collegiate swimmers ages 8 through 30.

Under 13: A parent or legal guardian must own the account and log in. The parent consents to collection of the child's profile, performance data, photos, and videos, and must approve AI video analysis before any video is sent for processing.

Ages 13–17: Athletes may use their own login; parents or guardians should supervise account creation and subscriptions where appropriate.

Ages 18–30: Athletes may use SwimIQ on their own account.

If you believe someone under 13 created an account without parental consent, contact privacy@swimiq.app and we will delete it.

8. Security
We use industry-standard transport encryption (HTTPS/TLS) and access controls. No method of transmission is 100% secure.

9. Your choices
• Edit or delete performance data within the app where supported
• Decline AI analysis consent (non-AI features remain available on eligible plans)
• Sign out to end your session on a shared device

10. International transfers
If you access SwimIQ from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the U.S. and other countries where our providers operate.

11. Changes
We may update this policy. We will revise the "Last updated" date and, where required, provide additional notice in the app.
