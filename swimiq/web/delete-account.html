Delete your account & data

SwimIQ · Account and data deletion requests · Last updated July 8, 2026

Request account deletion by email. We verify you control the account, then delete eligible data within 30 days.

Email deletion request

What we delete

  • Your sign-in account
  • Athlete profile information
  • Training logs, meet results, goals, and personal bests
  • Uploaded swim videos and AI analysis tied to your account
  • Subscription records for your account

How to request deletion

Email privacy@swimiqapp.com from the address on your SwimIQ account.

Subject: SwimIQ account deletion request

Include: account email, athlete/display name, and confirmation that you want deletion.

Accounts for children under 13: a parent or guardian must send the request.

Or email support@swimiqapp.com with the same details.
