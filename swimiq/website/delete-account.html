Delete your account & data

swimiqapp.com · Account and data deletion requests

Last updated: July 8, 2026

You can request deletion of your SwimIQ account and the personal data we store for that account. This page explains how.

What gets deleted

When we process a valid deletion request, we delete or de-identify, where possible:

  • Your account (email sign-in)
  • Athlete profile information you provided
  • Training logs, meet results, goals, and personal bests
  • Uploaded swim videos and AI analysis results tied to your account
  • Subscription records associated with your account

We may retain limited records where required by law (for example, billing or fraud-prevention records).

How to request deletion

Option 1 — Email (recommended)

Send an email from the address on your SwimIQ account to privacy@swimiqapp.com with the subject line SwimIQ account deletion request.

Include:

  • The email address you use to sign in to SwimIQ
  • Your display / athlete name (if different)
  • Confirmation that you want your account and associated data deleted

If the account holder is under 13, the deletion request must come from a parent or legal guardian.

Option 2 — Contact support

Email support@swimiqapp.com with the same information. We will route your request to our privacy team.

What happens next

  • We verify that you control the account (we may reply to the account email).
  • We delete eligible data within a reasonable time, usually within 30 days.
  • We email you when deletion is complete, when possible.

Before you delete

Deletion is permanent. Export anything you want to keep before requesting deletion. You can also delete individual training entries inside the app where supported.
