Everything in the app

📊

Dashboard

SwimIQ Score, progress charts, motivational cut badges, and quick links to your latest training and meet data.

🏊

Training log

Log practices by stroke, distance, course, and time. Edit or delete sessions anytime.

🏆

Meet results & PBs

Personal bests come from meet results only — official race times, not practice swims.

🎯

Goals

Set target times and dates; track progress against your current bests.

🇺🇸

USA Swimming standards

2024–2028 motivational time tables. Cuts use your passport birthday age and gender.

🛂

Athlete Passport

Recruiting-style profile: photo, GPA, website, interests, coach, school, strokes, and performance snapshot.

🧠

Race Intelligence

Meet-day checklist, warm-up plan, and nutrition guidance powered by SwimIQ AI from your own data.

📅

Schedule depot

Save meets, practices, and race times on the Dashboard and Log tab — your season in one place.

🎬

Video Lab

Upload swim videos. Optional SwimIQ AI coaching analysis with explicit consent before any video is sent.

⚙️

Settings & legal

Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and AI disclosure built into the app. Syncs securely across devices.
