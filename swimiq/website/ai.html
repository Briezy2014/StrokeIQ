SwimIQ AI & Data Disclosure Last updated: July 14, 2026 Required disclosure for Apple App Review Guideline 5.1.2(i) — third-party AI Web copy: https://swimiqapp.com/ai WHAT USES AI? • Video Lab — "Run AI Swim Analysis" on uploaded swim clips • Race Intelligence — meet-day plans and nutrition suggestions (generated on-device from your data; no video sent for nutrition text) • Future AI Coach features labeled "SwimIQ AI" WHAT DATA IS SHARED WITH THIRD-PARTY AI? When you run Video Lab AI analysis, SwimIQ sends to our secure backend: • The swim video file you uploaded • Swimmer context: event, stroke, distance, course, notes, goals, and relevant profile fields Our backend uses Google cloud AI services to analyze that content and return a coaching report. Google processes data as our subprocessor under their API terms. WHAT IS NOT SHARED WITHOUT YOUR ACTION? • Training log entries, meet results, and passport fields are not sent to Google unless you explicitly run AI video analysis or a future feature clearly discloses transmission. • On-device body mechanics (when available) are processed locally and are not uploaded as raw video for that step. CONSENT The first time you run AI video analysis, SwimIQ shows this disclosure and asks for your explicit permission. For athletes ages 8–12, a parent or legal guardian must provide this consent. You may decline and continue using non-AI features. REVOKE CONSENT Contact support@swimiqapp.com or delete your account to stop future AI processing. Clearing app data or signing out removes local consent flags on your device. ACCURACY AI output is estimated coaching feedback — not automatic video timing, not a substitute for your coach, and not medical or nutrition therapy.
